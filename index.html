Through our own struggles, we realized we were part of a community in need of COVID-19 vaccination appointments and sought to assist our eligible neighbors. Based on your outpouring of support, we know our work together has aided a few families and has given them a new sense of hope. With the progress of the rollout and increased availability, we are no longer a necessary community service. Stay safe, stay well, and love one another!
